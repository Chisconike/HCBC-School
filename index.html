OUR COURSES

Basic Theology

The course is one of the modules of Systematic theology. It integrates multiple subjects to provide an understanding of what the Bibles is, where it came from, how it was written and why it should be studied. The objective of the course is to provide an approach to studying the bible that will produce an accurate and unified understanding of scripture.

Ministerial Ethics

The Ministerial ethics - Orient new& experienced pastors on their roles& responsibilities and equipped with tools for effective ministry. The course deals with a Minister's relationship with his members,outsiders, and appropriate attitudes in given situations.

Hermeneutics

The Hermeneutics - The basic concepts of hermeneutics (the science and art of Bible interpretation) are taught in this course. Presentations of the laws of interpretation provide the student with a working knowledge of the exegetical process. Particular areas of emphasis are history, doctrine, culture, and parables.

Family Life

The Family life - Our courses deals with God's design for individual to enter into the institution of marriage, all aspects of family life, theological view of the family,the best foundation for the family.

The Bible Study Of Faith

The Bible Studies Of faith - The course reveals what bible faith is and what it is not, how faith comes, how to turn your faith loose and what it means to believe with your heart. The course again exposes the learner to the hindrances to faith and the God kind of faith.

Church And Missions

The Church & Missions - The School of Missions offers students education for effective leadership,in ministry, missions and beyond. It integrates biblical learning, theological training & musicological perspectives equips the graduates to make an impact in their unique context and calling.
